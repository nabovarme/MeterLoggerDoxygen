u16_t pbuf::ref

◆ tot_len

u16_t pbuf::tot_len

total length of this buffer and all next buffers in chain belonging to the same packet.

For non-queue packet chains this is the invariant: p->tot_len == p->len + (p->next? p->next->tot_len: 0)
